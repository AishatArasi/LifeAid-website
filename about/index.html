Who We Are

LifeAid is a compassionate online platform dedicated to helping terminally ill individuals raise funds for their medical treatment and organize community events that inspire hope and healing. We exist to make sure that no one has to face illness alone because together, we can turn empathy into action.

How LifeAid Works

LifeAid connects people who need help with those willing to give. Patients or their families can easily create verified fundraising campaigns, tell their stories, and receive donations directly through our secure platform. Beyond fundraising, LifeAid also helps communities organize charity events, awareness drives, and volunteer programs turning every effort into a ripple of change.

Our Vision

To build a compassionate world where every terminally ill person receives the care, dignity, and hope they deserve regardless of background or circumstance.

Our Mission

To provide life-changing support for terminally ill individuals through medical aid, emotional care, and community-driven initiatives connecting donors, volunteers, and healthcare partners to bring hope, healing, and humanity together.

Our Impact & Values

Since our founding, LifeAid has helped patients find hope in the midst of hardship and inspired individuals to take action through giving and volunteering.

Everything we do is guided by our core values:

  • Integrity
  • Compassion
  • Transparency
  • Community
  • Discretion
  • Accountability
  • Health
